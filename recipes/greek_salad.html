Greek Salad

Description

A Greek salad is a vibrant and refreshing Mediterranean dish that embodies the essence of summer on a plate. This classic recipe features a harmonious combination of crisp, fresh ingredients. Crisp cucumbers, plump, ripe tomatoes, and pungent red onions are skillfully chopped and mixed with briny Kalamata olives and creamy feta cheese. The salad is further elevated by aromatic oregano, zesty lemon juice, and a drizzle of extra-virgin olive oil, infusing each bite with a burst of flavor. The result is a delightful medley of textures and tastes, with the colorful vegetables providing a crunchy contrast to the richness of the cheese and olives. Greek salad is not just a dish; it's a celebration of the Mediterranean's sun-soaked flavors and the perfect accompaniment to any meal, offering a taste of Greece's culinary heritage

Ingredients

  • 2 large tomatoes, diced
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup Kalamata olives, pitted
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley leaves for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Start by preparing the vegetables. Dice 2 large tomatoes and 1 cucumber.
  2. Thinly slice 1/2 red onion.
  3. Pit 1 cup of Kalamata olives and set them aside.
  4. Crumbled 1 cup of feta cheese and keep it ready.
  5. In a small bowl, prepare the dressing by mixing 1/4 cup of extra-virgin olive oil, 2 tablespoons of red wine vinegar, 1 teaspoon of dried oregano, and the juice of 1 lemon.
  6. Season the dressing with salt and black pepper to taste.
  7. In a large salad bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, sliced red onion, and diced cucumber.
  8. Add the pitted Kalamata olives to the salad bowl.
  9. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the salad ingredients.
  10. Pour the prepared dressing over the salad.
  11. Gently toss the salad to ensure all ingredients are well coated with the dressing.
  12. Garnish with fresh parsley leaves if desired.
  13. Your classic Greek salad is ready to be served. Enjoy!
