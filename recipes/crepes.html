Crepes

Description

A classic crepe recipe is a delightful and versatile option for any meal of the day. To make these thin, delicate French pancakes, you'll need simple ingredients: all-purpose flour, eggs, milk, a pinch of salt, and a touch of melted butter. Combine these elements to form a thin batter, then pour it into a hot, lightly buttered skillet. Swirl the batter around to coat the pan evenly and cook until the edges turn golden brown. You can fill crepes with a variety of sweet or savory fillings like Nutella and bananas, strawberries and cream, or ham and cheese. With its endless possibilities, crepes are perfect for breakfast, brunch, or dessert, offering a taste of French cuisine that's easy to master at home.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • A pinch of salt
  • Melted butter

Steps

  1. Whisk together all-purpose flour, eggs, milk, and a pinch of salt in a bowl until a smooth batter forms.
  2. Stir in melted butter into the batter.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly butter it.
  4. Pour a small amount of the batter into the skillet and swirl it to coat the bottom evenly.
  5. Cook until the edges turn golden brown, then flip the crepe and cook the other side.
  6. Remove the crepe from the skillet and repeat with the remaining batter.
  7. Fill the crepes with your favorite sweet or savory toppings.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
