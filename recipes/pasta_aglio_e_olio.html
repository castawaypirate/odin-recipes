Pasta Aglio e Olio

Description

Pasta aglio e olio, a timeless Italian classic, is a simple yet extraordinary dish. It begins with al dente spaghetti or linguine, tossed in a luscious sauce crafted from garlic slices sautéed to a golden crisp in fragrant olive oil. The dish is elevated with a generous sprinkling of red pepper flakes for a subtle, warming kick, while fresh parsley adds a burst of vibrant color and herbaceous freshness. The magic of aglio e olio lies in its sheer simplicity, where every ingredient harmoniously blends to create a symphony of flavors that dance on your palate. It's a celebration of the beauty found in uncomplicated recipes, proving that with the right technique and quality ingredients, a few basic elements can result in a truly sublime culinary experience.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces (about 225 grams) of spaghetti or linguine
  • 4-5 cloves of garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • 1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional), for serving

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti or linguine until it's al dente, according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the extra-virgin olive oil in a large skillet over low heat.
  3. Add the thinly sliced garlic to the skillet and sauté it gently until it turns golden brown, taking care not to let it burn. This should take about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add the red pepper flakes to the skillet and sauté for an additional minute to infuse the oil with the heat from the chili flakes.
  5. Toss the cooked and drained pasta into the skillet with the garlic and chili oil, and then season it with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Toss to coat the pasta evenly with the flavorful oil.
  6. Garnish the dish with freshly chopped parsley for a burst of color and freshness.
  7. Serve your pasta aglio e olio hot, with optional grated Parmesan cheese for those who desire an extra layer of richness. Enjoy!
